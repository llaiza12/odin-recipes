Sauteed Green Beans

Description

Tasty green bean and beef dish mostly eaten in the Phillippines!

Ingredients

    Green beans

    Ground beef

    Soy Sauce

    Onions

    Garlic

    Tomatoes

    Salt and Pepper

    Water

Steps

    Cook beef and add garlic and onion after beef is browned.

    Add green beans, tomatoes, soy sauce, and salt and pepper.

    Add water and cook til done.
